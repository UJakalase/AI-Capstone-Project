The forge

Made slow,
on purpose.

Every blade in a batch is drawn from the same bar of steel, hammered by the same two hands, and quenched within the same hour. That's what "batch" means here — not a product line, but a single afternoon at the forge.

When a batch sells out, the pattern goes back in the drawer. The next one starts from a blank sheet — different steel, different handle stock, different reason for existing.
